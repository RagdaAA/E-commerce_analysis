# TechSphere

## Company Overview
TechSphere is a global direct-to-consumer e-commerce company specializing in consumer electronics, including computers, mobile phones, and audio accessories. The company sells exclusively through its online platform across four regions, allowing full control over pricing, promotions, and customer engagement.

As a data analyst at TechSphere, this analysis was conducted to help business stakeholders understand how sales performance and customer behavior have evolved over time, particularly during and after the COVID-19 pandemic. By examining historical sales data from 2019 to 2022, the goal is to identify what drove growth, which strategies were effective, and how TechSphere can maintain strong sales performance in a post-pandemic market.
